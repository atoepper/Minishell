
Minishell grammar, version I - LL(1) grammar, recursive descent parser (RDP) optimized 
<program>				->	{<pipe>}
<pipe>					->	<command term> {'|' <command term>}
<command term>			->	[<command>] [<redirection iteration>]
<command>				->	<execution path> {<argv>}
<redirection iteration>	->	<redirection term> {<redirection term>}
<redirection term>		->	<redirection> <file path> 
<redirection>			->	'<' | '>' | '>>' | '<<'
<file path>				->	word
<execution path>		->	word
<argv>					->	word

Minishell grammar, version II - using left recursion, thus leading to infite recursion with RDP
<program>				->	<pipe>
<pipe>					->	<command term> | <command term> <pipe>
<command term>			->	<command> <redirection iteration>
<command>				->	<execution path> <argv> | ε
<redirection iteration>	->	<redirection term> | ε
<redirection term>		->	<redirection> <file path> | <heredoc> <delimiter>
<redirection>			->	'<' | '>' | '>>'
<heredoc>				->	'<<'
<file path>				->	word
<execution path>		->	word
<delimiter>				->	word
<argv>					->	word | word <argv>
