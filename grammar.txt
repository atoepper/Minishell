
Minishell grammar, version III - LL(1) grammar, recursive descent parser (RDP) optimized 
<program>				->	<command term> {'|' <command term>}
<command term>			->	[<redirection iteration>] [<command>] [<redirection iteration>]
<command>				->	<execution path> {<argv>}
<redirection iteration>	->	<redirection term> {<redirection term>}
<redirection term>		->	<redirection> <file path> 
<redirection>			->	'<' | '>' | '>>' | '<<'
<file path>				->	word
<execution path>		->	word
<argv>					->	word
